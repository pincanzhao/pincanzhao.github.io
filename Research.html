Research Interests

My research interests span the intersection of intelligent transportation systems, artificial intelligence, and security, focusing on developing innovative solutions for next-generation transportation. Current research directions emphasize efficient and secure cooperative perception frameworks for connected and autonomous vehicles (CAVs), leveraging advanced machine learning techniques and privacy-preserving mechanisms.
