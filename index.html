Brief Biography

  • Pincan Zhao received dual Ph.D. degrees in Information Technology from Carleton University, Ottawa, Canada, in 2025, and in Information and Telecommunication Engineering from Xidian University, Xi’an, China, in 2025. He received his B.Eng. degree in Telecommunication Engineering from China University of Petroleum, Qingdao, China, in 2019. Since December 2025, he has been a Postdoctoral Associate with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Faculty of Engineering, Western University, London, ON, Canada, working with Prof. Kelly Yili Tang. His research interests span the intersection of intelligent transportation systems, artificial intelligence, and security, focusing on developing innovative solutions for next-generation transportation. His current research directions emphasize efficient and secure cooperative perception frameworks for connected and autonomous vehicles (CAVs), leveraging advanced machine learning techniques and privacy-preserving mechanisms. He has published over 30 research articles in top-ranked journals and conference proceedings, including IEEE TITS, IEEE TMC, IEEE TVT, IEEE IoT Journal, and IEEE TDSC. He received the Best Paper Award at IEEE FCN 2024 and ACM DIVANet 2023. He received the Western Postdoctoral Fellowship Awards for 2025-2027. He is a Member of IEEE.
