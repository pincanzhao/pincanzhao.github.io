Teaching

I believe the best way to teach demanding engineering subjects is to turn abstract concepts into intuition that students can reason with, by grounding every idea in concrete, real-world problems they can build and test.

My teaching experience spans graduate and undergraduate levels at three institutions, including an English-taught UK joint program, and consistently connects classroom concepts to real connected-vehicle and IoT systems. I value clear problem framing, hands-on labs, and mentoring students from diverse backgrounds.
